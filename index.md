I am a math graduate doing machine learning. Over the past 8 years I've worked across research and engineering teams at Microsoft Research, Adobe, Rephrase.ai and Goldman Sachs. During my time at Microsoft Research, I published a [paper at KDD](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/publication/generalized-zero-shot-extreme-multi-label-learning/) (a leading AI conference) and while at Adobe, I co-invented a US patent in computer vision. As a core contributor to [Adobe's production lipsync generation system](https://research.adobe.com/video/dubbing-lip-sync/), I ran training experiments for its audio-to-video model. I have also released open-source projects like [Mamba SSM for macOS](https://github.com/purohit10saurabh/mamba-ssm-macos) and [minFLUX](https://github.com/purohit10saurabh/minFLUX).

The math and machine learning stuff I write about are in this [technical blog](/). Having travelled to 15 countries, here are some of my [travel experiences](/travel/).

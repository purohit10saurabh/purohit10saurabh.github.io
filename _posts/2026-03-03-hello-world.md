This is a sample post to show how math rendering works.

<!--more-->

Inline math works with dollar signs: the energy-mass relation is $E = mc^2$.

Display math uses double dollars:

$$
\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} \, dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
$$

You can write matrices:

$$
A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{bmatrix}
$$

And summations:

$$
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}
$$

Everything is just markdown with LaTeX between `$...$` or `$$...$$`. Edit this file on your phone via GitHub to add new posts.

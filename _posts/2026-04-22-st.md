# My findings in solo travel
You apply yourself a lot in solo travel. Those decisions are with stakes. For example, you carry the things you pack for a trek. You have to predict what you would need there and pack exactly those things. If you carry useless things, it will become your burden. And if you miss a necessary thing, then you are in trouble. Hence you learn about your preferences and limitations thereby getting better at decison-making and travelling.
